Shop Mod

The Shop Mod is a modification that allows a universal player-accessible shop with items that can vary throughout the game at the whim of the game-maker.

Here's an arbitrary list that may constitute a shop.

  • Sword : 30G
  • Health Potion : 20G
  • Grandmaster's Poop: 50000G

Note: This mod is extremely flexible, so I can't really elaborate more on this. Oh, and the shop can be used by any player at any time, unless you're playing with the Hyper-Realism mod active.
